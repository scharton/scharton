Welcome to The Bar

This is a growing list of favorite drinks sampled in bars and experimented at home.

Manhattan

  1. Chill a martini glass in the fridge
  2. 6-8 ice cubes in a pint glass
  3. Add 2-3 oz bourbon or rye
  4. Add 1/2-1 oz vermouth
  5. 3 dashes of bitters
  6. Stir or swirl glass for 20-30 seconds
  7. Strain into chilled martini glass

Old Fashioned (no fruit)

  1. Start with a small glass
  2. Add 1/4 oz simple syrup or a 1/2 tsp of sugar
  3. Add 3-4 dashes of bitters
  4. Muddle if using sugar
  5. Add some ice cubes, large if available
  6. Add 2-3 oz bourbon
  7. Stir and drink

Sour

  1. Prep in a shaker
  2. Put ice in the shaker
  3. Add 3 oz fresh lemon juice
  4. Add 1/2 oz simple syrup
  5. Add 2-3 oz bourbon
  6. Shake agressively for 20 seconds
  7. Strain into a chilled glass or a glass with ice
